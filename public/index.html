Hangman Game - How to Play

Welcome to the Hangman Game! The objective of the game is to guess the hidden word by suggesting letters one at a time. Each incorrect guess will bring you closer to being "hanged." You have a limited number of incorrect guesses before the game is over. Good luck, and have fun playing!

Rules:

  • A word will be randomly selected.
  • You guess one letter at a time.
  • Correct letters appear in the word.
  • Wrong guesses add a part to the hangman.
  • If the hangman is fully drawn before you guess the word, you lose.
  • Try to guess the word with as few mistakes as possible!
